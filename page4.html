Chinmay Ananda B.E. (Civil), MBA, international speaker, financial educator and pricing strategist is the proud owner of six failed businesses. He’s proud to have failed so many times, as it spurred him to find out the reasons why. Researching hundreds of businesses and understanding what caused them to fail, he soon launched his Finance Academy.

Since 2010, Chinmay has taught easy to understand financial management to over 700 business owners and non-finance managers.

This all led to his writing Amazon.com Bestseller, FUNamentals of Financial Statements. Chinmay explains, educates and demonstrates how small businesses can avoid financial trouble, learn how to read numbers to keep a pulse on their business and above all, make business finance FUN to learn!

Chinmay is an internationally renowned keynote speaker, available to speak on a variety of business finance topics for your next presentation, event or seminar.

Chinmay is a member of the Professional Speakers Association, winning their prestigious Kerrie Nairn Scholarship for Public Speaking in 2016.

He has presented talks to major corporations, business networks and renowned conferences both in Australia and around the world.
